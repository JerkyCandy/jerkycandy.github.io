About

JerkyCandy is a solo indie game developer based in South Korea. As a solo developer, JerkyCandy works on various aspects of game development, including design, programming, and implementation. The focus is on creating gameplay-driven experiences and exploring new ideas through independent game projects.

Creative Direction From concept to execution, each project is built around a distinct gameplay identity and a strong player-facing idea.
End-to-end solo development Planning, programming, and implementation are all done by one person alone, from the first idea to the finished build.

FOURVIVE

FOURVIVE is an online co-op survival action game for up to four players who cooperate, use a wide range of weapons, and work together to take down enemies as they appear.

  • 4-player online co-op Up to four players join the same battles online.
  • Survival action combat Fast-paced combat built on cooperation.
  • Diverse weapon system Many weapon types to suit how you like to play.
